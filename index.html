SLOGAN: FARMING FOR DEVELOPMENT

OJAPH AGRO DEVELOPMENT LIMITED (OADL) is an Agricultural Development firm started in March 2021 and got incorporated in Uganda on the 10th of November, 2022 providing farmers with access to quality, genuine and affordable Agricultural Inputs, Agricultural Extension Services and Market Linkages to profitable and reliable output. We operate in the West Nile region of Uganda and our office is at Kubala, Terego, Arua city, Uganda

Our Products & Services

  1. Agricultural Inputs
  2. Agribusiness and Agriculture Consultancy Services
  3. Commodity Trading
  4. Trainings on General Agronomy.
